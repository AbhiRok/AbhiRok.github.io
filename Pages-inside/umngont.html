Description

Flowing gracefully through the scenic landscapes of Meghalaya, the Umngot River is a crystal-clear gem of the Northeast. Its pristine waters, renowned for their exceptional clarity, reveal a kaleidoscope of colorful stones and aquatic life beneath the surface. As the lifeline of Dawki village, the Umngot not only provides sustenance but also offers unforgettable experiences like boating and river diving. Encircled by lush greenery and towering cliffs, this tranquil river beckons travelers to immerse themselves in its enchanting beauty and serenity.
